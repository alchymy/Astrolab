Circles and Spheres
 

  • Equation and Calculation



\[ Circumference \; C = 2 \pi r \]
\[ Sphere Volume \; v = \frac{4}{3} \times \pi \times r^3 \]
\[ Circle Area \; a = \pi \times r^2 \]
\[ Sphere Area \; a = 4 \times \pi \times r^2 \]
where r is the radius of a circle or sphere.

Variables / Constants:
  • r is the radius in m
  • C is the Circumference in m
  • v is the sphere volume in m3
  • a is area in m2
