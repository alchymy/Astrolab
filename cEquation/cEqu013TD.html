Time Dilation
 

  • Equation and Calculation
  • Notes



\[ dt = t0 \times \sqrt{ 1 - \frac{v^2}{c^2}} \]
Variables / Constants:
  • dt is time dilation
  • t0 is elapsed time on stationary clock
  • v = velocity between stationary and moving reference frames
  • c = speed of light
If someone traveled 99.9% of the speed of light for 1 year. How many years would have passed on earth? Answer 7.08 years.
