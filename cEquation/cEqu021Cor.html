\[ V = \frac{D \pi [ cos(N) - cos(F) ] }{T} \]
Variables / Constants:
  • D is the diameter of the planet in km
  • T is the rotational period time in hours
  • N is the latitude nearer the equator
  • F is the latitude further from the equator
Example: Earth diameter 12760 km, Rotational period 24 hrs, N = 30 deg. and F = 31 deg. The value of V is 4.1 m s-1.
