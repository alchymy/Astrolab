This utility permits the user to convert degrees between decimal degrees and degrees, minutes, and seconds and visa versa, for latitude and longitude calculations.

1 degree = 60 arcmin = 3600 arcsec =  (π / 180) radians.  1 radian = (180 / π) degrees = 206265 arcsec link to std values page
